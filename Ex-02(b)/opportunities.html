This degree programs prepares students for Advanced, Technical Computer System Design and Development Work. It includes the study of Database Design, Data communications, Procedural and Object Oriented Programming, Operating Systems Design, Algorithm Development and Applications of Artificial Intelligence. The curriculum includes basic course work in Electronics Technology and Theory of Programming Languages. Students focus on developing computer-based solutions involving hardware and software components and integration.
